About Fur Buddies

Fur Buddies Adoption Center is dedicated to rescuing cats and dogs from bad situations/environments, and make sure they are moved into loving forever homes. We believe every animal deserves love and a second chance at happiness. Don't wait any longer, get your own fur buddy today.

Our Mission

  • Rescue abandoned dogs & cats
  • Provide medical care and shelter
  • Match pets with loving families
  • Promote responsible adoption

Why Adopt?

Adopting a loving cat or dog, saves their lives and fills them and you with endless happiness. Adopting also helps reduce shelter overcrowding, since we rescue big groups of cats and dogs each week.
